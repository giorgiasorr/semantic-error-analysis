# Analyzing the Moses Illusion: A Data-Driven Study of Semantic Errors

## Overview
The experiment focused on the Moses illusion, a phenomenon in which individuals fail to notice an inconsistency or error in a question or statement, even when they know the correct information. For this experiment, 74 individuals answered a series of general knowledge questions. The findings of this study confirmed that the participants failed to detect the illusion.
